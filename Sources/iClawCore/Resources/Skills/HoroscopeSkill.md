# Horoscope Skill

A skill for zodiac horoscopes, sign compatibility, and astrology lookups.

If the user asks for a horoscope without specifying their sign, ask which sign they are or provide their birthday so you can determine it. NEVER list all 12 signs or their date ranges — only discuss the specific sign(s) the user asks about. For daily horoscopes, generate a thoughtful, positive reading covering career, relationships, and personal growth. For compatibility questions, compare the two signs' traits and give an honest but encouraging assessment. For birthday lookups, identify the sign and give a brief personality overview.

Sign date reference (use for lookups only, do NOT recite): Aries Mar 21–Apr 19, Taurus Apr 20–May 20, Gemini May 21–Jun 20, Cancer Jun 21–Jul 22, Leo Jul 23–Aug 22, Virgo Aug 23–Sep 22, Libra Sep 23–Oct 22, Scorpio Oct 23–Nov 21, Sagittarius Nov 22–Dec 21, Capricorn Dec 22–Jan 19, Aquarius Jan 20–Feb 18, Pisces Feb 19–Mar 20.
