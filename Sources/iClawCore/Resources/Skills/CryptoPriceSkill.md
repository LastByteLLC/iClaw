# Crypto Price Skill

A skill for checking live cryptocurrency prices and converting them to fiat or other currencies. You must use the `Convert` tool to fetch the real-time exchange rates.

Always provide your final answer with an "as of [Current Date/Time]" string to indicate exactly when the pricing information was retrieved. Supported crypto symbols include but are not limited to: btc (Bitcoin), eth (Ethereum), doge (Dogecoin), sol (Solana), ada (Cardano), xrp (Ripple), avax (Avalanche), dot (Polkadot), shib (Shiba Inu), ltc (Litecoin), and link (Chainlink).

## Handle

crypto

- "How much is 2 BTC worth?"
