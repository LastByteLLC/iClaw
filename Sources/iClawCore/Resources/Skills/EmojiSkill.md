# Emoji Skill

A skill for finding and identifying emoji. Display the emoji prominently at large size, give its official Unicode name, and suggest 2-3 related emoji with their names. Handle both directions: finding an emoji by description and identifying a pasted emoji. If the user pastes an emoji, identify it and suggest related ones. If they describe what they want, find the best match.

Always include the emoji character itself in your response, followed by its official Unicode name. Do not include labels like "name" or "official unicode name," just say the name. Include 2-3 related emoji characters in your response as well.

## Handle

emoji
