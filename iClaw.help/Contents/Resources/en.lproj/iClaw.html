iClaw Help

Your on-device AI assistant for macOS

iClaw is an AI-powered assistant that lives in your menu bar. It uses Apple Intelligence to run entirely on your device — your data never leaves your Mac.

Click the claw icon in the menu bar to open the floating HUD, then type or speak your request.
